Call Preparation

How to Get the Most From Your Strategy Call

1

Know Your Numbers

Come prepared with your target investment amount and your timeline for deploying capital. This helps our team tailor the conversation to your specific situation.

2

Write Down Your Questions

Whether it is about returns, tax benefits, hold period, risk profile, or our track record. Bring every question. There are no bad ones, and we would rather address them directly than have you leave with uncertainty.

3

Include Decision Makers

If you make investment decisions with a partner or spouse, have them join the call or be available to review the details afterward. The best outcomes happen when all stakeholders are aligned.

This call is for you if:

  • You are an accredited investor (income $200K+ individual or net worth $1M+ excluding primary residence)
  • You have at least $50,000 available to invest
  • You are comfortable with a 3 to 5 year hold period
  • You prioritize track record and operator credibility over aggressive projections
  • You are ready to make a decision within 30 to 90 days

This call is not for you if:

  • You are not an accredited investor
  • You are looking for guaranteed returns with no risk
  • You do not have capital available to deploy in the near term
  • You are expecting liquid access to your investment at any time
  • You are just browsing with no intention of taking action

If any of the "not for you" criteria apply, please reschedule. This call will not be the right fit, and we would rather save both of our time.
