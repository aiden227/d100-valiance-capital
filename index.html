The Problem

The Problem With Most Syndication Sponsors

Most accredited investors looking for passive real estate deals end up in one of three traps.

The Overpromiser

Glossy pitch decks with aggressive IRR projections. No operational experience. No skin in the game. The deal closes, communication stops, and the returns never materialize.

The Outsourcer

The sponsor buys the property but hands everything off to a third-party property manager. When something goes wrong, they have no control. You are left hoping the market bails out a deal nobody is actively managing.

The One-Hit Wonder

A sponsor with one or two successful deals who is now raising for a $50 million fund. No track record through a downturn. No systems. No bench depth. Your capital is their experiment.

The result is the same: underperforming returns, zero transparency, and investors who swear off syndications entirely.
